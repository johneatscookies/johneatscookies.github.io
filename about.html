CHS SCIOLY

Cerritos High School SOF has now competed in Science Olympiad for over 4 years, winning regional titles and reaching state.

Our achievements include:

  • Over 100 Medals, including several at State.
  • 3rd at Los Angeles Regionals 2019.
  • Multiple entrances into the Southern California state competition in the last 4 years.
