ABOUT
CHS SCIOLY

Cerritos High School SOF has now competed in Science Olympiad for over 4 years, winnning regional titles and reaching state.

Our achievements include:

  • Over 100 Medals
  • 3rd at Los Angeles Regionals 2019
  • Went to State Twice
